Bisnakandi:

Bisnakandi, near Sylhet on the India-Bangladesh border, offers a stunning blend of green hills, clouds, stones, and crystal-clear waters from the Meghalaya Mountains. Visitors can relax on the rocks or enjoy a peaceful swim. The Border Hut market, open every four days, sells mountain fruit, clothes, and cosmetics. The best time to visit is from September to March. Although there are no hotels in Bisnakandi, nearby Sylhet city offers accommodations!

Warning

Caution is advised during the monsoon due to fast-flowing water. Caution is also advised when walking in the water, because of slippery stones.
